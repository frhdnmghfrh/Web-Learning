Tips & Articles

Tips for Memorizing English Vocabulary

  • Use apps or interactive games to learn vocabulary in a fun way.
  • Use mnemonic methods like acronyms: STORE = Strawberry, Tomatoes, Olives, Rice, Eggs.
  • Create pictures or storyboards to visualize the meaning of new words.
  • Make flashcards for solo or group practice.
  • Read English media and write down new vocabulary.
  • Write down new words on sticky notes and review them often.
  • Create sentences using new vocabulary to understand the context.
  • Practice speaking with the new words.
  • Stick vocabulary around visible spots like your desk or wardrobe.
  • Memorize gradually: 5–8 words per day.

How to Practice English Conversation

  • Improve your vocabulary and pronunciation.
  • Be brave to speak and overcome shyness.
  • Practice talking to yourself to build confidence.
  • Find supportive partners to practice speaking with.
  • Use movies to learn expressions and intonation.
  • Use apps to speak with others around the world.
  • Do role-plays or simulations with friends.
  • Talk about topics you enjoy to stay motivated.
  • Try to use English in your daily life.
  • Imitate native speakers to improve accent and pronunciation.
