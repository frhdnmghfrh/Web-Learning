Theme 1: Ordering Food at a Restaurant (Beginner)

Waiter: Good evening, welcome to our restaurant. How many?
Customer: Table for two, please.
Waiter: Right this way. Here are the menus.
Customer: Thank you. (After a few minutes) I’d like to order.
Waiter: Are you ready to order, sir/madam?
Customer: Yes, I’ll have the spaghetti carbonara and a glass of iced tea.
Waiter: And for you, madam?
Customer: I’ll take the grilled salmon with mashed potatoes and a lemonade.
Waiter: Okay, spaghetti carbonara, grilled salmon, iced tea, and lemonade. Anything else?
Customer: No, thank you.
Waiter: Alright, please wait for a moment.

Kosakata Penting:

  • Table for two: Meja untuk dua orang
  • Menu: Daftar makanan dan minuman
  • I’d like to order: Saya ingin memesan
  • Spaghetti carbonara: Spaghetti carbonara
  • Grilled salmon: Salmon panggang
  • Mashed potatoes: Kentang tumbuk
  • Iced tea: Es teh
  • Lemonade: Limun

Theme 2: Vacation at the Beach (Intermediate)

Friend A: Hey, have you decided where to go for our vacation this summer?
Friend B: I’m thinking of going to Bali. I’ve heard the beaches are amazing there.
Friend A: Bali sounds great! I’ve always wanted to go there. What activities do you want to do?
Friend B: Definitely swimming and sunbathing. And I want to try surfing.
Friend A: That sounds like fun! We should also visit some temples and explore the local culture.
Friend B: Absolutely! We can rent a scooter and explore the island at our own pace.
Friend A: Perfect! Let’s start planning the details, like flights and accommodation.

Kosakata Penting:

  • Vacation: Liburan
  • Accommodation: Akomodasi
  • Sunbathing: Berjemur
  • Surfing: Berselancar
  • Temples: Kuil
  • Local culture: Budaya lokal
  • Scooter: Sepeda motor
  • Island: Pulau

Theme 3: Job Interview (Advanced)

Interviewer: Good morning, please have a seat.
Candidate: Good morning, thank you.
Interviewer: So, tell me a little about yourself.
Candidate: Certainly. I’m a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science from the University of XYZ. I have experience in software 
development and a passion for problem-solving.
Interviewer: That’s great. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Candidate: My strengths include my ability to work well in a team and my attention to detail. As for weaknesses, I sometimes struggle 
with delegating tasks because I like to ensure everything is done perfectly.
Interviewer: I see. Where do you see yourself in five years?
Candidate: In five years, I hope to be a senior software engineer, leading projects and mentoring junior developers.
Interviewer: Alright, do you have any questions for me?
Candidate: Yes, what opportunities are there for professional development within the company?
Interviewer: We offer various training programs and encourage employees to pursue certifications.
Candidate: That sounds excellent. Thank you for your time.
Interviewer: Thank you for coming in. We’ll be in touch.

Kosakata Penting:

  • Graduate: Lulusan
  • Software development: Pengembangan perangkat lunak
  • Problem-solving: Pemecahan masalah
  • Strengths: Kekuatan
  • Weaknesses: Kelemahan
  • Delegating tasks: Mendelegasikan tugas
  • Senior software engineer: Insinyur perangkat lunak senior
  • Mentoring: Pembimbingan
  • Professional development: Pengembangan profesional
  • Certifications: Sertifikasi

Theme 4: Shopping at the Supermarket (Beginner)

Customer: Excuse me, where can I find the milk?
Staff: The milk is in aisle 4, next to the dairy products.
Customer: Thank you. And where are the fruits?
Staff: The fruits are in the produce section, near the entrance.
Customer: Great, thanks for your help.
(At the checkout)
Cashier: Hello, do you have a loyalty card?
Customer: Yes, here you go.
Cashier: That’s $25.50, please.
Customer: Here’s $30.
Cashier: Here’s your change, and your receipt. Have a nice day!
Customer: Thank you, you too!

Kosakata Penting:

  • Aisle: Lorong
  • Dairy products: Produk susu
  • Produce section: Bagian produk segar (buah dan sayur)
  • Loyalty card: Kartu pelanggan
  • Checkout: Kasir
  • Change: Kembalian
  • Receipt: Struk

Talking About the Weather (Beginner)

Person A: What’s the weather like today?
Person B: It’s sunny and warm. Perfect for a picnic!
Person A: That sounds great. Should we go to the park?
Person B: Definitely! But bring sunscreen.
Person A: Good idea! And maybe a hat too.
Person B: Let’s do it!

Kosakata Penting:

  • Sunny: Cerah
  • Warm: Hangat
  • Picnic: Piknik
  • Sunscreen: Tabir surya
  • Hat: Topi

Theme 6: Giving and Asking for Directions (Intermediate)

Tourist: Excuse me, can you tell me how to get to the museum?
Local: Sure, go straight ahead until you see the traffic lights, then turn left.
Tourist: Left at the traffic lights. Got it.
Local: Then walk for about two blocks. You’ll see a park on your right. The museum is just across the street from the park.
Tourist: Okay, so it’s on the left side across from the park?
Local: Exactly. You can’t miss it.
Tourist: Thank you so much for your help!
Local: You’re welcome! Have a great visit!

Kosakata Penting:

  • Go straight ahead: Jalan terus
  • Traffic lights: Lampu lalu lintas
  • Turn left: Belok kiri
  • Blocks: Blok (satuan jarak)
  • Across the street: Di seberang jalan
  • You can’t miss it: Anda tidak akan melewatkannya

Theme 7: Making a Doctor’s Appointment (Intermediate)

Receptionist: Good morning, this is Dr. Smith’s office. How can I help you?
Patient: Hello, I’d like to make an appointment to see Dr. Smith, please.
Receptionist: Have you been here before?
Patient: No, this is my first time.
Receptionist: Okay, what’s your name and date of birth?
Patient: My name is John Doe, and my date of birth is January 1, 1990.
Receptionist: Alright, what’s the reason for your visit?
Patient: I’ve been having a bad cough and a fever for a few days.
Receptionist: Okay, let me see. The earliest appointment we have is next Tuesday at 10 AM. Does that work for you?
Patient: Yes, that’s fine.
Receptionist: Okay, so that’s John Doe on Tuesday at 10 AM. Please arrive 15 minutes early to fill out some paperwork.
Patient: Great, thank you!
Receptionist: You’re welcome! See you then.

Kosakata Penting:

  • Make an appointment: Membuat janji
  • Date of birth: Tanggal lahir
  • Reason for your visit: Alasan kunjungan
  • Cough: Batuk
  • Fever: Demam
  • Paperwork: Administrasi (formulir)

Theme 8: Talking About Favorite Movies (Advanced)

Friend A: Have you seen any good movies lately?
Friend B: I recently watched "Parasite" and it was absolutely amazing!
Friend A: Oh, I’ve heard about that one! What’s it about?
Friend B: It’s a South Korean film about class differences and social inequality. The plot twists are incredible, and the acting is superb.
Friend A: It sounds intriguing. I’ll add it to my watchlist.
Friend B: Definitely do! And what about you? Any recommendations?
Friend A: I saw "Nomadland" a few weeks ago. It’s a beautiful and poignant film about a woman traveling through America after losing everything in the recession.
Friend B: That sounds like something I would enjoy. I appreciate films that make you think.

Kosakata Penting:

  • Plot twists: Alur cerita yang tak terduga
  • Intriguing: Menarik
  • Watchlist: Daftar tontonan
  • Poignant: Menyentuh
  • Recession: Resesi
  • Class differences: Perbedaan kelas
  • Social inequality: Ketidaksetaraan sosial
  • Superb: Luar biasa
